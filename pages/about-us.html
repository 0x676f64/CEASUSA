About Us

Cutting Edge Automotive Solutions (CEAS) is a customer-oriented equipment sales, service, and training company, specializing in OEM and specific aftermarket diagnostic tools and equipment for cars and light-duty trucks, agriculture and construction equipment, heavy-duty diesel and light commercial vehicles, marine vessels, and powersports vehicles.

CEAS is the exclusive importer of SP Tools and the registered agent for the US. We are also the US Master Distributor for TEXA, a worldwide leader in professional diagnostics.

Our mission is to enhance the lives of everyone we come in contact with. We are committed to leading the way in how professional tools and equipment are sold and serviced, setting the standard for quality and customer satisfaction.

Master Distributor Opportunities Available

Are you a successful entrepreneur looking to add to your portfolio? CEAS is looking for distribution partners across the US. If you're a professional distributor or an entrepreneur with experience running other successful businesses, contact us today.
