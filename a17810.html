Product Description

The KONFORT 780 is fully automatic and makes managing R1234yf and R134a quick and easy. Once you have selected the make and model, the Konfort machine automatically performs all the necessary operations. With the fastest changeover time between different refrigerants, this is the fastest dual-gas machine on the market. This is due to minimal shared components between refrigerant types, which means these are two complete machines in one compact package.

  • Fully automatic R134a and R1234yf Management
  • Best in class 10" touchscreen allows for intuitive and fast operation
  • Innovative dual-stage vacuum pump and large compressor allow for the fastest operation in the industry
  • Reports can be printed via WiFi or an optional thermal printer
  • Automatic refrigerant and contamination detection prevent damage to your investment
  • Monitor machine status from anywhere in the shop via mobile app
  • Integrated vehicle capacity database
  • Automatic maintenance alarm and simple filter access mean less downtime
  • Remote technical support and training through TeamViewer
  • Highly efficient refrigerant recovery of over 97% means less waste of expensive refrigerant
  • Automatic management of non-condensable gasses
  • 2 x 20 lb. refrigerant tanks
  • Multi-Language software
