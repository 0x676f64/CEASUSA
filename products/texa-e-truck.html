ATTENTION ALL DRIVERS AND FLEET MANAGERS

eTruck is an innovative miniaturized tool that, for the first time, takes heav-duty truck workshops into a new diagnostic dimension.


With this simple tool, you can remotely monitor the vehicle status, manage the servicing from a predictive point of a view, and perform adjustment functions that allow you to restore the vehicle's optimal conditions.

REMOTE DIAGNOSIS ANYWHERE

eTruck, installed in the vehicle’s diagnostic socket and configured within minutes, acts as a conjunction element between the repairer and the heavy-duty vehicle. This ability will increase customer loyalty thanks to a constant and absolutely competent assistance service.

FLEET MANAGERS CAN:

Check their vehicles' maintenance status/service deadlines, and provide remote assistance for their fleet using their workshop's diagnostic and troubleshooting functions. Fleet managers can also share a calendar of service appointments with the workshop, monitor the style of all drivers, and download tachograph data/trip data directly from the control unit in real-time.

MACHANICS CAN:

Continuously and remotely monitor the status of vehicles and work on their electronic systems as if they were in the workshop. They also can arrange ordinary and extraordinary maintenance in advance, share their appointments calendar with drivers and fleet managers, manage their entire customer bases with the same software and create detailed profiles for each customer.
